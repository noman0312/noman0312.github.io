Approach

Teaching philosophy

I teach by connecting equations and theory with physical behavior observed in measurements, waveforms, and hardware. In laboratory settings, I focus on helping students understand why real systems deviate from ideal models, how to debug systematically, and how to communicate technical findings clearly.

Strengths in the lab
  • Clarifying the link between theory and measured results
  • Supporting structured troubleshooting during practical work
  • Helping students develop confidence with instruments and experimental method
  • Explaining circuit and converter behavior in accessible technical language
